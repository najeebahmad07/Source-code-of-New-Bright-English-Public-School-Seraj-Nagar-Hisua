About Us

Welcome to New Bright English Public School, a place where young minds are nurtured and prepared for a bright future. Founded by Md Sadique Hussain in 2017, our school is dedicated to providing high-quality education in a supportive and caring environment.
Located in Seraj Nagar, Hisua (Nawada), with the pin code 805103, New Bright English Public School aims to foster academic excellence and personal growth in all our students. Our experienced and dedicated teachers work tirelessly to ensure that each child receives the attention and guidance they need to succeed.
We believe in a holistic approach to education, where academic learning is complemented by extracurricular activities that promote physical, social, and emotional development. Our well-rounded curriculum is designed to inspire a love for learning and to encourage critical thinking, creativity, and problem-solving skills.
At New Bright English Public School, we are committed to creating a safe and inclusive environment where every student feels valued and respected. We strive to instill strong moral values and a sense of responsibility in our students, preparing them to become thoughtful and engaged citizens.
Join us at New Bright English Public School and be a part of our mission to shape the leaders of tomorrow.

Our Vision

○Academic Excellence: Every student achieves their full academic potential through a rigorous and engaging curriculum that fosters critical thinking and a love for learning.
○Holistic Development: Students grow not only intellectually but also socially, emotionally, and physically, through a variety of extracurricular activities and a supportive learning environment.
○Inclusive Community: We create an inclusive and respectful community where every student feels valued, safe, and supported, regardless of their background or abilities.
○Global Citizenship: Students are prepared to become responsible and compassionate global citizens, equipped with the knowledge and skills to make a positive impact in the world.
○Innovative Learning: We embrace innovative teaching methods and technologies to enhance learning experiences and prepare students for the challenges of the 21st century.

Our Mission

✔ Empower Students: Equip students with the knowledge, skills, and confidence to excel academically and personally.
✔ Foster Lifelong Learning: Cultivate a passion for learning that encourages continuous growth and curiosity beyond the classroom.
✔ Promote Values: Instill strong ethical values, respect, and a sense of responsibility in our students to guide their actions and decisions.
✔ Encourage Creativity: Inspire creativity and innovation through diverse and dynamic educational experiences.
✔ Build Community: Create a nurturing and inclusive school environment that fosters strong relationships among students, teachers, and the community.
